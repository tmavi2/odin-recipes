Colombian Arepas

"This recipe is so good and so easy," says community member jande2mom.

My daughter first found it as part of a school project

but we have since made it just for our family, because it is easy and delicious!

I will say the arepas are much better fresh. They don't keep their taste as well

after a few days in the fridge, but that is really a problem since we usually devour them

all on the same day!"

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup pre-cooked white corn meal
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • cooking spray

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Mix water, corn meal, mozzarella cheese, butter, and salt together in a large bowl.

    Knead until mixed well and the dough has a soft consistency. Form balls the size of a

    medium orange and place them between 2 sheets of plastic wrap. Flatten with a

    rolling pin to your desired thickness.

  2. Step 2

    Cut the dough into circles using a cereal bowl or drinking glass, lip-down, through the

    plastic wrap. Remove the plastic wrap and remove excess dough.

  3. Step 3

    Coat a griddle with cooking spray and heat to medium-high. Add arepas and grill unti

    golden brown, about 5 minutes per side. Serve immediately.
